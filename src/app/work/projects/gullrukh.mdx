## Overview

Development of a customized eCommerce platform tailored to specific business needs, focusing on performance, usability, and scalable architecture. The platform was built to offer a seamless shopping journey while giving administrators full control over products, orders, and customer interactions.

## Key Features

- **Custom Product Management**: Dynamic product creation with variants such as sizes, colors, and pricing rules.
- **Personalized Shopping Experience**: User-friendly interface with custom filters, search, and recommendations.
- **Shopping Cart & Checkout**: Secure cart handling and streamlined checkout process.
- **Order & Inventory Management**: Real-time tracking of stock levels and order statuses.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Custom admin panel for managing products, users, and system configurations.
- **Secure Authentication**: User login and session handling for secure transactions.

## Technologies Used

- **Flask**: Lightweight Python backend framework for building fast and scalable web services.
- **Bootstrap**: For responsive and mobile-first UI design.
- **PostgreSQL**: Relational database for reliable and structured data storage.

## Challenges and Learnings

A major challenge was optimizing database queries and managing complex product relationships. This project strengthened skills in schema design, performance tuning, and building secure authentication flows with Flask.

## Outcome

The platform was successfully delivered in **early 2025**, providing a stable, scalable, and customizable eCommerce solution. It improved business operations, enhanced customer experience, and supported future growth through its flexible architecture.
