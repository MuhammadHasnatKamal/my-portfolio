## Overview

Development of a service-focused website that provides heating, boiler installation, and maintenance services. The platform was designed to help customers easily book services online and connect with technicians through a clean and user-friendly interface.

## Key Features

- **Online Booking System**: Customers can schedule appointments for heating and boiler services directly through the website.
- **Service Management Pages**: Dedicated pages for boiler installation, boiler repairs, and routine maintenance services.
- **Contact & Inquiry Forms**: Built-in forms for customer inquiries and quote requests.
- **Mobile-Responsive Design**: Optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices to ensure accessibility across all devices.
- **SEO Optimization**: Structured content for better visibility on search engines.

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress**: Content management system used to build and manage the website.

## Challenges and Learnings

A key challenge was building a smooth and reliable booking experience while keeping the admin management simple. This project improved knowledge of WordPress plugin configuration, performance optimization, and securing contact forms against spam and unauthorized access.

## Outcome

The website was successfully completed in **November 2024**, helping the business streamline customer inquiries and manage service bookings efficiently. It improved customer engagement and made it easier for clients to book heating and boiler services online.
